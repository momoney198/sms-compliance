Privacy Policy

Summer Brain Boost · Last updated: June 10, 2026

The short version

Summer Brain Boost does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Period.

What the app stores

Student names (as entered by you), quiz answers, streaks, and progress are stored only on your device, using Apple's standard local storage. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or to anyone else, and is deleted if you delete the app.

What the app does not do

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no user accounts, no third-party SDKs, and makes no network requests of its own.

External links

Some ASL questions include optional links to free video lessons on Lifeprint.com. Tapping a link opens your web browser and takes you to that third-party website, which has its own privacy practices. The app itself sends nothing to these sites.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children. There are no accounts, no social features, and no in-app purchases.

Changes to this policy

If the app's privacy practices ever change, this page will be updated before the change takes effect.
