Summer Brain Boost — Support

Daily summer practice for AP®-style Calculus and Physics, ASL, and SAT® prep: short daily quizzes, longer study sessions, step-by-step solutions, and concept lessons for when you're stuck.

Frequently asked questions

How do I add a student? Tap the gear icon → type a name under Students → Add. Each student keeps their own subjects, streaks, and progress.

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes the data.

Can I study just one topic? Yes — open a subject, choose a Topic under Study Session, and start a focused set.

Does the daily quiz reset? Fresh questions arrive each day per subject. Finishing all of a subject's daily questions extends that subject's streak.

Why do ASL questions link to another website? Sign language is best learned by watching. Those links go to free video lessons at Lifeprint.com (Dr. Bill Vicars).
