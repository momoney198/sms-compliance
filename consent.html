The opt-in flow (screenshot)

The operator opts themselves in by running a private command-line setup script on their own Mac. The script displays the full consent disclosure (brand name, purpose, message frequency, msg & data rates notice, STOP/HELP instructions, privacy policy URL) and prompts for the mobile number. Exact consent language shown to the operator

"By entering your mobile number below, you consent to receive automated account-activity SMS alerts from your own trading software, sent ONLY to this number. Message frequency varies with trading activity. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help. Privacy policy: https://momoney198.github.io/sms-compliance/privacy.html Terms: https://momoney198.github.io/sms-compliance/sms-terms.html"

What happens after consent

The number is stored in the operator's local macOS Keychain. It is never transmitted anywhere except to Twilio's API for delivery of the operator's own alerts. There is no database, no server, no list, no third party.

No other opt-in paths exist

No web form. No SMS keyword opt-in. No purchased list. No affiliate. No third party. The only number that will ever receive messages on this campaign is the operator's own number, entered by the operator on their own machine through the screen shown above.
