Mo Realty Group — SMS / Text Message Privacy Policy

Mo Realty Group operates a private, automated text-message notification program that delivers account-activity alerts from personal trading software to the account owner's own mobile number.

No sharing of mobile information

No mobile information (phone numbers or consent data) collected for this SMS program is sold, rented, or shared with any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. This information is used solely to deliver the notifications described above.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies and depends on trading activity.

Rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out / help

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts; reply HELP for assistance.
